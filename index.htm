With New Horizons coming out soon, I've finally finished documtened the total 26 e-Reader cards that contain town tunes.
The ones in the first section  , were e-Cards that were released in English & Japanese.
 

The ones in the second section , were e-Cards that were only released in Japanese.


So, what I did was take screenshots of the town tune e-reader screens in both Japanese & English and made a translation chart.


With the card-e+ town tunes, I matched the hex # color of the english note with the original Japanese note to make sure it was the correct one. I then recreated it on the Island Tune website to make sure it matched up. I link those on each page that needs it with this icon . I also include the original screenshot just to be sure. If there were any mistakes, please let me know.
